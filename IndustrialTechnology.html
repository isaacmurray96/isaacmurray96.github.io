Industrial Technology

Throughout my educational and practical journey, I have consistently held a strong fascination for industrial technology. This passion began during my school years, where I excelled in the subject and even achieved a Band 5 for my HSC timber major work. My enthusiasm carried forward into university, where I earned a High Distinction for my timber-focused unit, showcasing my deep understanding of the field.

In practice, I have translated my knowledge into hands-on creations, crafting an array of distinctive timber furniture pieces. My proficiency in timberwork is evident through my ability to conceptualize, design, and construct functional and aesthetically pleasing pieces. While my primary focus has been on timber, I have also dabbled in metalwork, mastering the fundamental techniques.

Though my metalwork experience is limited, I have established a solid foundation by grasping the basics. This well-rounded expertise, spanning both timber and metal, has equipped me with a unique skill set that allows me to blend creativity with technical proficiency.

As I continue to explore the world of industrial technology, my journey thus far underscores my dedication, adaptability, and commitment to honing my craftsmanship. With a background rooted in excellence and a passion for innovation, I eagerly anticipate further opportunities to contribute to and excel in this dynamic field.
