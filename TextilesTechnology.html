Textiles Technology

My journey into textiles technology began with an open mind and a willingness to explore new horizons. Although I had no prior experience with textiles before university, I embraced the challenge presented by the compulsory textiles unit in my first year. This initial exposure ignited a newfound interest, leading me to make the decision to pursue textiles as a minor.

Throughout my academic journey, I delved deeper into textiles technology, immersing myself in various projects that showcased my evolving skills. These projects have been diverse, and I've even ventured into working with leather, which added an exciting dimension to my experience. The tactile nature of working with textiles resonated with me, and I found immense joy in both the hands-on processes and the creative design aspects of the subject.

In essence, my venture into textiles technology has been a story of discovery and passion. From a humble beginning of unfamiliarity, I've grown to appreciate the artistry and technicality of this field. As I continue to learn and create, I am fueled by the satisfaction of transforming raw materials into tangible, beautifully designed pieces.
