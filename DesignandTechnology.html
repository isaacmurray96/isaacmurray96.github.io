Design and Technology

I developed a passion for design and technology during my high school years when I chose it as an elective in both Year 11 and 12. Immersing myself in this field, I thoroughly enjoyed the creative process and the challenges it presented. This dedication culminated in an impressive achievement when I received a Band 5 for my major work in the HSC.

My enthusiasm for design and technology carried over to my university studies, where I continued to excel. Throughout my design and technology classes at university, I consistently earned High Distinctions (HDs) for my grades. These successes reinforced my commitment to honing my skills and expanding my knowledge in this dynamic and innovative field.

Overall, my journey through design and technology has been marked by a consistent drive for excellence and a genuine passion for the creative and technical aspects of the discipline.
